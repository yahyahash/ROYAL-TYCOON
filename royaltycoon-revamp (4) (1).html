About Us

A Legacy of
Diversified
Excellence

RoyaltyCoon Group of Companies is a Dubai-based limited liability conglomerate that encompasses a diverse range of businesses across twelve distinct industries. This structure allows for flexibility and protection of personal assets while enabling each subsidiary to operate under a unified brand.

"Empowering communities through sustainable practices, we commit to making a positive impact beyond our business."
Our Approach
Diversified strategy that mitigates risks and capitalises on different market opportunities.
Our Structure
Each business maintains its own identity while benefiting from shared resources and expertise.
Our Base
Headquartered in Ras Al Khor Industrial Area, Dubai — the heart of global commerce.
Our Reach
Active operations across the UAE and expanding into new global markets by 2026.
